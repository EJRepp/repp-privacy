Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Repp ("we", "our", or "the app") is a gym workout tracker developed by Eric Justiz. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address and display name to create your account.

Workout Data: Exercises, sets, reps, weights, body weight entries, progress photos, body measurements, and goals you log in the app. This data is stored locally on your device and in our cloud database (Firebase) to enable features like the community forum and friend challenges.

Health Data: If you enable Apple Health integration, we read body weight data and write workout summaries to Apple Health. This data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers.

Community Content: Forum posts, comments, and upvotes you make are stored in our database and visible to other users.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to provide app functionality: tracking workouts, displaying progress, enabling social features, and syncing with Apple Health. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Third-Party Services

Firebase (Google): We use Firebase for authentication and data storage. See Firebase Privacy Policy.

RevenueCat: We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. See RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

Expo: We use Expo for push notifications. See Expo Privacy Policy.

Data Storage and Security

Most of your data is stored locally on your device. Data that powers social features (forum posts, friend connections, challenges) is stored securely in Firebase. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

Your Rights

You can delete all your data at any time from the Settings screen in the app. You can also request data deletion by contacting us.

Subscriptions

Repp Pro is available as a monthly ($4.99/month) or yearly ($29.99/year) auto-renewable subscription. Payment is charged to your Apple ID account. Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage subscriptions in your Apple ID settings.

Children's Privacy

Repp is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
